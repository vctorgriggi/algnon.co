<h3 class="not-prose mb-1 text-lg font-medium">some more about me</h3>
<span class="not-prose text-muted-foreground text-xs">
  last updated: <time datetime="2025-12-10">December 10, 2025</time>
</span>

algnon starts from a particular way of looking at things: taking them apart, understanding how they move, putting them back together. not as a technique, but as a way to notice the slow shifts that usually go unseen.

the idea is simple: pay attention to the space between intention and outcome. that's where ideas take shape, where small decisions carry weight, where creation leaves traces that aren't always visible.

algnon works as that lens. it has no biography, no figure behind it. it exists to explore rhythm, clarity, adaptation, and the subtle ways the world responds to what we build.

it's a place for slow thinking in an environment that insists on speed.
